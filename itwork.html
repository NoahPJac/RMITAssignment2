Introduction

For our IT Work competent we elected to interview a Jack Chang a software developer based down in Coffs Harbour. Jack has worked on several fantastic projects such an example is the online Naplan test, where he worked as part of a team to create the online components. Jack has really provided us with some fantastic insight into the world of IT development.

We have created a summary overview of our interview with him as well as compiled a list of the top questions and answers that we posed to him. Finally, we have created a detailed explanation of this new information relates to the group and our possible future careers.

  3. Can you explain your team composition and your role within the team?

Take care of things like making sure we're designing things that look correct, making sure we will have our requirements correct. Make sure they're creating the right stuff, and then we have testers that make sure that it's all working properly.

  4. Can you break down how a project goes from concept to finished state?

    In simplest terms, we get a contract one way or another. Then we figure out what our product is missing to meet their needs, which then broken up into different epics (groups of work) then those are broken up into stories (specific singular pieces of work) those are then refined by a team of developers to add the specifics on how to add those to the product. We then take the refined stories into each sprint (2-week gap of work) until we feel like we have as much work as we can fully complete (i.e. shippable) in that sprint, we then repeat that process until we have sprints planned until the work is done (this process is repeated while in motion, so we might plan the 2nd sprint halfway through the first, then the 3rd halfway through the second, etc). So as an example for RMS we might need: to meet their needs tests and give them answers. This means we need the epics: Add video questions, mark answers upon completion of the answer (not at the end of the test), and export answers For the video questions epic we'd have stories like Add a new way to create video questions Show video questions to the user Mark video questions automatically Add video question responses to our reports
